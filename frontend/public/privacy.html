Ottertest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-11

Ottertest is a meeting recorder that transcribes and summarizes audio you record. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. Ottertest is self-hosted: the operator who deployed this instance runs it inside their own cloud account and is the data controller for your data.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and password, used to sign in (managed by Amazon Cognito).
  • Audio recordings — audio you explicitly record or capture in the app, using your device microphone (and, only if you enable it, audio from a browser tab you choose to share).
  • Derived content — transcripts, summaries, action items, folders, and speaker names generated from or added to your recordings.
  • Basic technical data — standard request metadata (e.g. timestamps) needed to operate the service.

How we use it

  • To record, transcribe, summarize, and display your meetings back to you.
  • To let you organize, rename, search, and delete your own content.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your recordings and metadata are stored in the operator's own Amazon Web Services (AWS) account — audio in Amazon S3 (private, encrypted) and metadata in Amazon DynamoDB. Access is restricted to your authenticated account; users only ever see their own meetings.

Third-party processors

To produce transcripts and summaries, audio and text are sent to:

  • Deepgram — receives your audio to produce the transcript. See Deepgram's privacy policy.
  • Groq — receives the transcript text to produce the summary and action items (only if AI summaries are enabled). See Groq's privacy policy.

These providers process the data to return a result to Ottertest; they are not permitted to use it for their own purposes under their terms.

Microphone permission

The app requests microphone access only while you are recording, to capture the audio you choose to record. Recording never starts without your action.

Data retention & deletion

  • You can delete any meeting at any time from the app; this removes its audio and metadata.
  • To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact the operator at the address below.

Children

Ottertest is not directed to children under 13 and should not be used by them.
